# Modern Design Improvements Applied

## What Changed

### 1. **Bigger, Better Spacing**
- Increased padding from `p-6` to `p-8`
- Increased gaps from `gap-6` to `gap-8`
- Larger header icon badges (16x16 instead of 10x10)
- More breathing room throughout

### 2. **Enhanced Shadows & Depth**
- Upgraded to `box-shadow: 0 20px 25px -5px` (much deeper)
- Gradient backgrounds within sections (gray-50 to white, blue-50 to white)
- Better border styling with rounded-2xl corners

### 3. **Modern Form Inputs**
- Larger inputs: `px-5 py-4` (instead of px-4 py-3)
- Rounded-xl corners (more modern)
- Bigger text: `text-base font-medium` 
- Focus states with colored borders and subtle shadows
- Custom dropdown arrows using inline SVG
- Interactive focus effects (border changes to section color)

### 4. **Better Typography**
- Headers: `text-xl font-bold` (instead of text-lg font-semibold)
- Labels: `font-bold` with better contrast
- Larger icons in headers (text-2xl)
- Better letter spacing
